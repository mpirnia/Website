Other Research

Additional research in energy systems, optimization, and stochastic methods

Research Summary

Additional research areas include robust optimization, stochastic programming, and applications of operations research to energy and infrastructure systems. This encompasses sustainable aviation fuel supply chains, energy system planning under uncertainty, and interdisciplinary projects at the intersection of engineering and management.
